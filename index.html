About Miraz High School

Miraz High School is committed to academic excellence, character formation and community service. We offer a broad curriculum that balances STEM, arts, sports and leadership.

Mission

To empower students with knowledge, integrity, and the courage to lead.

Vision

A community of lifelong learners that shapes a brighter future.

Values

  • Respect
  • Discipline
  • Curiosity

Academics & Programs

Science & Technology

Practical labs, coding clubs, and project-based learning.

Arts & Humanities

Creative expression through music, drama and visual arts.

Sports & Wellness

Physical education, inter-house competitions and wellness programs.

Admissions

We welcome applications for the next academic session. Scholarships and sibling discounts available.

  1. Complete online application form.
  2. Submit previous school records and birth certificate.
  3. Attend entrance assessment and interview.
