Admissions

Welcome to the Miraz High School admissions page. We are delighted that you are considering joining our family of learners.

How to Apply

  1. Download and fill the application form from our office.
  2. Submit the form with required documents and passport photographs.
  3. Wait for interview and entrance test schedules.
  4. Successful applicants will be contacted by phone or email.

Requirements

  • Birth Certificate
  • Recent passport photographs
  • Previous academic report
  • Transfer certificate (if applicable)
